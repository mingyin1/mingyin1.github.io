Research Experiences

Robust Federated Learning Mitigates Client-side Data Inference Attacks
    Advisor: Prof. Neil Gong, Duke University
  • Introduced InferGuard, an innovative defense designed to protect against client-side inference attacks on FL.
  • Proposed adaptive attack using PGD.
  • Conducted extensive experiments and found InferGuard effectively mitigates malicious clients’ attack, outperforming all other baselines.
Poisoning Federated Recommender Systems with Fake Users
    Advisor: Prof. Neil Gong, Duke University
  • Concluded the extra knowledge required by existing attacks on federated recommender systems(FedRecs).
  • Introduced PoisonFRS, a novel poisoning attack on FedRecs that need no any extra knowledge of FedRecs.
  • Conducted extensive experiments and found that PoisonFRS is effective even when the proportion of fake users is extremely low, a scenario in which all other existing attacks proved ineffective.
Secure Large Language Model (Graduation Project)
    Advisor: Prof. Weiming Zhang, USTC
